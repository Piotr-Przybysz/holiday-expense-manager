Holiday Expense Manager 2.0 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-07

Holiday Expense Manager 2.0 (HEM 2.0) helps users plan trips, track expenses, store receipts, review OCR text, manage budgets, and share travel data.

Data stored by the app

The app may store the following user-created data:

  • Trips and trip notes
  • Expenses and budgets
  • Participants and local user profile
  • Stays/accommodation and timeline information
  • Exchange rates and currencies
  • Receipt attachments and recognized OCR text
  • Sharing/collaboration metadata
  • Local diagnostic logs

Local storage and iCloud

Data is stored locally on the user's device. If iCloud features are used, data may synchronize through the user's iCloud / CloudKit account. Shared trips use Apple's iCloud Sharing.

Sharing

When a user shares a trip, invited collaborators may receive access to that trip through Apple's CloudKit Sharing. A .hemshare file is only a preview and does not grant access.

Analytics, advertising and tracking

The app does not sell user data. The app does not use advertising. The app does not intentionally include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Diagnostics

Diagnostic logs are stored locally. They are not sent automatically. The user may manually copy or share them for support.
