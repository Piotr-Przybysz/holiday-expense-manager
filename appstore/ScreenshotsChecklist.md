# Screenshots Checklist

Do not submit placeholder screenshots. Capture real screenshots from the App Store build.

## App Store Connect requirements reminder

- iPhone: prepare at least one supported iPhone screenshot set, preferably the largest currently required/accepted size such as 6.9" or 6.5"/6.7" depending on App Store Connect.
- iPad: if iPad is supported, prepare iPad screenshots.
- Screenshots should be JPG or PNG.
- App Store Connect accepts 1–10 screenshots per supported size.

## Suggested iPhone screenshots

1. Home with active trip.
2. Trip detail screen.
3. Receipt-first expense entry.
4. OCR review.
5. Budget / budget split.
6. Reports with chart.
7. iCloud Sharing screen.
8. Diagnostics/logs — optional for review notes, not necessarily public App Store marketing.

## Suggested iPad screenshots

1. Home / trip list.
2. Trip detail with sections.
3. Expense entry with receipt/OCR layout.
4. Reports.
5. Sharing.

## Review-only screenshot ideas

- iCloud Sharing screen.
- App logs / diagnostics showing local logs can be copied.
