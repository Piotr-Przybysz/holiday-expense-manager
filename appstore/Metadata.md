## Description

Holiday Expense Manager 2.0 helps you organize travel costs from the first plan to the final settlement.

Create trips, add expenses, scan receipts with local OCR, manage participants, track stays, define budgets, record exchange rates, and review reports. The app is designed for real travel: quick expense entry, receipt-first workflows, offline-friendly manual entry, and clear budget summaries.

Key features:

- Trip planning with dates, notes, participants and stays.
- Fast expense entry from the home screen or trip details.
- Receipt attachments and local OCR text capture.
- Budgets and budget usage summaries.
- Multi-currency expenses and exchange-rate handling.
- Participants, payers and settlement foundation.
- Reports with expense breakdowns.
- iCloud sync for the user's Apple devices.
- iCloud Sharing foundation for shared trips.
- JSON/ZIP backup foundation and local diagnostics.

Data is stored locally and can sync through the user's iCloud account. `.hemshare` files are previews only; real shared access is granted through iCloud Sharing.
